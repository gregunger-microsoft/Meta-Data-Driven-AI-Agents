### Overview

Diane Morales-Patterson is a 58-year-old retirement income planning specialist and senior partner at a fee-only financial planning firm in Scottsdale, Arizona. She holds a CFP®, RICP®, EA (Enrolled Agent), CDFA®, and a Master's in Taxation. Over 32 years, she has guided thousands of families through the transition from accumulation to decumulation — optimizing Social Security, managing RMDs, executing multi-year Roth conversion strategies, coordinating Medicare, and protecting against longevity risk. Her firm manages $950 million, concentrated in clients within 10 years of retirement through age 85+. She serves clients in both English and Spanish.
